Charles Gasparino is an American journalist and a blogger. He frequently serves as a panelist on the Fox Business Network program segment The Cost of Freedom and the stocks/business news program Cashin’ In. Gasparino also worked as a senior writer in Newsweek. He is an occasional radio host.

The journalist was born on January 28, 1962, in Bronx, New York, USA. First from his family to attend college, he graduated with a B.A. from Pace University and earned a Master’s Degree in Journalism from the University of Missouri in Columbia, Missouri.

Gasparino joined Fox Business Network in 2010 after he left CNBC. He is a very talented presenter and is the only journalist included in The Daily Beast’s top 15 Economic and Business Commentators.

Charlie Gasparino Prostate Cancer Illness Update

Charlie Gasparino revealed that he was diagnosed with prostate cancer a month ago. A few weeks before being diagnosed, Gasparino caught COVID but had only mild symptoms.

The blood test showed a spiking PSA level which served as a marker of cancer. So a day after a biopsy, the doctor revealed that he was diagnosed with malignant prostate cancer.

In a New York Post column, Charlie Gasparino has written that people need to listen to the science and check out with medical professionals. In addition to the doctors, the journalist has thanked all the staff of the NYU hospital where he was treated on.

As of now, the journalist has posted on Twitter that his recovery process is going very well and his health is returning to normal.

No official word has come from the wife of the journalist regarding his diagnosis and treatment of prostate cancer. The journalist has told that his wife urged him to be on top of his bloodwork.
