Billy Bob Spouse

Billy Bob Thornton and Connie Angland have been married since 2014. Connie is an actress as well as a former special effects crew.

Connie was born on 22 October 1964 to her parents, Angie Murch and James Angland. Angie married the reputed film director, Walter Murch in 1965.

Walter and Angie raised Connie in Bolinas, California. She has a brother named Walter Slater Murch and sisters named Carrie Angland and Beatrice Louise Murch.

As per IMDb, Angland joined the entertainment sector as an animatronic engineer in 1990. Following that, she became special effects artist for movies like Prehysteria 2 and Big Momma’s House.

In 2002, Connie portrayed a short role in Puphedz: The Tattle-Tale Heart. She was uncredited for her appearance in Jayne Mansfield’s Car in 2012.

    Cynda Williams

    Pietra Dawn Cherniak

    Thornton tied the knot with his fourth spouse Pietra Dawn Cherniak on 18 February 1993. They welcomed two children and split in 1997.

    Pietra is a former Playboy model who has been featured in several magazines. She was a part of the documentary series E! True Hollywood Story in 2005.

    Dawn and Billy’s marriage created chaos in the media. They drew and maintained attention to themselves for a long time.

    Angelina Jolie

    Angelina Jolie and Billy married on 5 May 2000. They starred together in Pushing Tin in 1999 and formed an association.

    Their relationship did not last long as they split in June 2002. Angelina and Thornton separated legally on 27 May 2003.

    Jolie is 20 years younger than Billy. According to GQ, the couple split because Thornton felt inadequate in front of one of the most tattoed celebrities Angelina.

    The actress liked going out and was involved with United Nations and other agencies. However, Billy would just sit and watch baseball at home.

    Due to these differences, Billly felt less which fueled the couple’s high-profile split.
