Espeon  
エーフィ Eifie
For more information on this Pokémon's species, see Espeon.

Espeon (Japanese: エーフィ Eifie) is a Psychic-type Stage 1 Pokémon Prime card. It is part of the Undaunted expansion.

Card text

Evolution Memories
しんかのきおく
 
Espeon can use the attacks of all Pokémon you have in play that evolve from Eevee as its own. (You still need the necessary Energy to use each attack.)
Solar Ray
ソーラーレイ		30
Remove 1 damage counter from each of your Pokémon.

Release information

This card was released in the English Undaunted expansion. In Japan, it was released as a L-P Promotional card and was awarded alongside Umbreon from March 2010 as a Special Rank prize to members of the Pokémon Daisuki Club. To achieve a Special Rank, players had to accumulate at least 7,200 Action Points (AP).
